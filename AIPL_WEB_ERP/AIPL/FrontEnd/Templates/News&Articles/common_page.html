Life at Agratas Infotech

At Agratas Infotech, we pride ourselves on fostering a workplace that is dynamic, inclusive, and inspiring. Located in the heart of Noida at Ocean Plaza, Sector 18, our office is a hub of innovation and creativity. With a core team of 154 experts and a total strength of over 214, including interns and management, we are a close-knit yet expanding community that thrives on collaboration and excellence.

We offer competitive hiring packages, with salaries reaching up to 25 Lakhs CTC, reflecting our commitment to attracting and retaining top talent. Our hybrid work model provides flexibility, allowing team members to balance work and life effectively, whether they are in the office or working remotely. Office hours are from 10 AM to 6 PM, ensuring a healthy work-life balance.

Agratas Infotech is more than just a tech company; we are a partner in your professional growth. We provide extensive internship opportunities, sponsorships, and scholarships, empowering the next generation of tech leaders. We believe in investing in our people, offering continual learning and development through hands-on project management, consultancy, and specialized training programs.

Our work spans across various domains, including project management, consultancy, banking, and business solutions, enabling us to impact a wide array of industries. This diverse expertise ensures that no two days are the same at Agratas, keeping our work exciting and challenging. Our team's diverse background brings a wealth of knowledge and experience, fostering an environment where innovation is not just encouraged but is a part of our DNA.

Life at Agratas is a blend of professional development and personal growth. We encourage team members to take part in recreational activities, knowledge-sharing sessions, and regular team-building events. Whether it’s a casual game in the break room, a team outing, or participating in our annual tech fest, we ensure there’s always a balance between work and fun.

If you’re looking to start or advance your career in a place that values your contributions and supports your ambitions, Agratas Infotech is the place to be.
